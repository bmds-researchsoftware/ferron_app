Learn to cope
What are coping skills?
We all have ways that we like to deal with difficult feelings. These are ways that we cope or deal with difficult situations or feelings.
Why learn more to quit?
In order to quit smoking, we need to use a lot of these skills. Many people think they are just using "will power", to quit when really, they have things that they do to cope with difficult situations and feelings.
What does this app do?
We want you to learn new ways to cope with uncomfortable and difficult feelings related to quitting. So we've taken the best scientific knowledge and more than a little common sense to teach you some new methods. We want this time you quit to be a success!
